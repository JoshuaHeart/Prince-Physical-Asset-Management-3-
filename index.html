General Business Activities

Prince Physical Asset Management Services embeds the science of sustainable asset management in the operations of large industry and ultimately it improves bottom-line profits, safety and the lives of our citizens. It is for medium to large industrial customers such as power and water utilities, chemical industries and municipalities. The result of poor asset management has been a loss of millions of rands, major power outages, water shortages and even extends to bridges collapsing. The consequence of this has been job losses, reduction in foreign investment and risk to the lives of citizens and our workforce. Prince Physical Asset Management Services is unlike other asset management and maintenance management offerings in the market in that the service it provides not only provides you with the management systems for you to implement but it provides you with key insight into the sustainability of your current operations and whether you are heading for, or why you are experiencing, major failures.
